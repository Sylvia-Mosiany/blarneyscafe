Blarney's Cafe is Willimantic's original Irish Cafe. Located on High Street which is off of Main Street, Blarney's is a place where local dining meets excellent customer service. We pride ourselves in sourcing local produce from farmers in Eastern Connecticut. Our practices are sustainable and conducted with most care and hygiene. We have weekly specials available at the cafe.
